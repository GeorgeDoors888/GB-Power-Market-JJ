### How to Access
1. Visit the SP Energy Networks website: [https://www.spenergynetworks.co.uk/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.aspx](https://www.spenergynetworks.co.uk/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.aspx)
2. Click on the links which will redirect you to Scottish Power's website
3. Alternatively, go directly to Scottish Power's website: [http://www.scottishpower.com/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.asp](http://www.scottishpower.com/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.asp)
4. Navigate to the "SP Manweb" section and select the relevant year (2025, 2026, etc.)

**Note**: Scottish Power's website has security measures that prevent automated downloads. All files must be downloaded manually through a browser.

## 2. National Grid Electricity Distribution (formerly Western Power Distribution)

National Grid Electricity Distribution (NGED) covers four DNO regions. Due to website restructuring, the document library URLs have changed. The current files can be found by visiting their main website and navigating to the charging documents section.

### Manual Access for All Regions

The National Grid Electricity Distribution DUoS charging documents for all regions can be found at:
[https://www.nationalgrid.co.uk/electricity-distribution/](https://www.nationalgrid.co.uk/electricity-distribution/)

Navigate to:
1. Document Library
2. Charging and Connections
3. Distribution Use of System Charges

Then filter by region:

### East Midlands (MPAN 11)

#### Documents to Look For:
- **Schedule of Charges and Other Tables**: East Midlands Schedule of Charges and Other Tables
- **CDCM Model**: East Midlands CDCM model

### West Midlands (MPAN 14)

#### Documents to Look For:
- **Schedule of Charges and Other Tables**: West Midlands Schedule of Charges and Other Tables
- **CDCM Model**: West Midlands CDCM model

### South Wales (MPAN 21)

#### Documents to Look For:
- **Schedule of Charges and Other Tables**: South Wales Schedule of Charges and Other Tables
- **CDCM Model**: South Wales CDCM model

### South West (MPAN 22)

#### Documents to Look For:
- **Schedule of Charges and Other Tables**: South West Schedule of Charges and Other Tables
- **CDCM Model**: South West CDCM model

**Note**: National Grid has recently restructured their website, and direct document URLs have changed. Manual navigation through their website is required to access these documents.

## 4. Other UK DNOs

The remaining UK DNOs have their own websites where DUoS charging methodologies can be accessed:

- UKPN (UK Power Networks) - Eastern (MPAN 10), London (MPAN 12), South Eastern (MPAN 19)
- NPG (Northern Powergrid) - Northeast (MPAN 15), Yorkshire (MPAN 23)
- ENWL (Electricity North West) - North West (MPAN 16)

## Notes on Document Access

All DNO websites have security measures that prevent automated downloads. Manual download through a browser is required for all files:

1. **National Grid Electricity Distribution**:
   - Visit: [https://www.nationalgrid.co.uk/electricity-distribution/](https://www.nationalgrid.co.uk/electricity-distribution/)
   - Navigate to the document library and search for relevant charging documents

2. **SP Manweb**:
   - Visit: [http://www.scottishpower.com/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.asp](http://www.scottishpower.com/pages/connections_use_of_system_and_metering_services.asp)
   - Navigate to the SP Manweb section
   - Click on the required documents

3. **SSEN**:
   - Visit: [https://www.ssen.co.uk/our-services/use-of-system-and-connection-charging/](https://www.ssen.co.uk/our-services/use-of-system-and-connection-charging/)
   - Navigate to the appropriate section for SHEPD or SEPD
