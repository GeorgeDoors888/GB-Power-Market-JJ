# T_HUMR-1 Generation and Trading Analysis Report
September 15, 2025

## Executive Summary
Analysis of T_HUMR-1's generation and trading patterns from 2022 to September 2025 reveals significant insights into their operational strategy and market behavior.

## Generation Patterns

### Volume Trends
1. **Increasing Generation Trend**:
   - Starting from relatively low volumes in 2023 (~200-800K MW monthly)
   - Significant increase in 2024 (2-4M MW monthly)
   - Dramatic increase in 2025 (reaching 9.5M MW in August 2025)

2. **Notable Generation Stops**:
   - January 2023: -126,918 MW
   - November 2023: -10,570 MW
   - January 2024: -558,760 MW
   - January 2025: -399,156 MW

### Price Analysis

1. **Offer Prices (Selling)**:
   - Highest: 257.66 GBP/MWh (January 2023)
   - Lowest: 107.20 GBP/MWh (April 2024)
   - Recent Average (2025): ~135 GBP/MWh

2. **Bid Prices (Buying)**:
   - Range: -2,300 to -2,500 GBP/MWh
   - Most aggressive buying in February 2024 (-2,474.82 GBP/MWh)

## Strategic Generation Stops Analysis

### Profitable Generation Stops
1. **January 2023**:
   - Volume: -126,918 MW
   - Market Context: High offer prices (257.66 GBP/MWh)
   - Likely profited from buying back at lower prices

2. **January 2024**:
   - Volume: -558,760 MW
   - Strategic timing with lower market prices (132.21 GBP/MWh)
   - Largest strategic stop in the dataset

3. **January 2025**:
   - Volume: -399,156 MW
   - Higher offer prices (189.93 GBP/MWh)
   - Continued pattern of January strategic stops

### Seasonal Patterns
1. **Winter Strategy**:
   - Regular generation stops in January
   - Higher offer prices during winter months
   - Lower volumes but more strategic trading

2. **Summer Operations**:
   - Consistently high generation volumes
   - More stable pricing
   - Focus on steady generation rather than strategic stops

## Trading Strategy Analysis

### Price Spread Optimization
- Average spread between bid and offer prices: ~2,300-2,600 GBP/MWh
- Wider spreads during periods of market volatility
- Narrower spreads during stable generation periods

### Volume Management
1. **2023**: More frequent small volume adjustments
2. **2024**: Increased baseload generation with strategic stops
3. **2025**: Significant volume increase with maintained flexibility

## Conclusion
T_HUMBR-1 demonstrates a sophisticated trading strategy with three key patterns:

1. Strategic generation stops in January of each year
2. Increasing overall generation volume year-over-year
3. Maintaining wide bid-offer spreads for trading flexibility

The unit appears to be most profitable during planned generation stops, particularly in winter months when prices are higher. The strategy of stopping generation and buying back at lower prices has been consistently applied, with the largest such operation in January 2024.

## Recommendations
1. Consider extending strategic stops to other winter months besides January
2. Maintain higher offer prices during winter periods
3. Continue the trend of increasing base generation volume
4. Monitor the success of the August 2025 high-volume strategy
