## File Type Categories

### 1. **Schedule of Charges** (Primary Tariff Documents)
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Content**: Annual DUoS tariff rates by customer category, voltage level, time band
- **Frequency**: Annual (April publication for following year)
- **File Size**: Typically 1-5 MB

### 2. **CDCM Models** (Common Distribution Charging Methodology)
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Content**: Detailed calculations for tariff derivation
- **Purpose**: Transparency in methodology
- **File Size**: Typically 5-20 MB

### 3. **PCDM Models** (Primary Cost Distribution Model)
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Content**: Primary substation cost allocation
- **DNOs**: SPEN (SPM, SPD), SSEN (SHEPD, SEPD)

### 4. **ED2 PCFM** (Price Control Financial Model)
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Content**: ED2 (2023-2028) regulatory price control calculations
- **DNOs**: SPEN, NGED
- **File Size**: 10-50 MB

### 5. **Annex Files** (Supplementary Data)
- **Format**: CSV, Excel
- **Content**: Detailed rate tables, time-of-use schedules
- **Examples**: LV/HV charges, embedded network rates

### 6. **Regulatory Documents**
- **Format**: PDF
- **Content**: Special conditions, flexibility services statements
- **Purpose**: Compliance and transparency
